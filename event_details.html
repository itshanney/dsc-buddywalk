Sunday, October 4, 2015
1:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m.
Seattle Center, Next50 Plaza

The Down Syndrome Community is proud to welcome honored guest, Dr. Karen Gaffney as the Keynote Speaker at the 2015 Puget Sound Buddy Walk®. Karen is known as a long distance swimmer, president of a non-profit organization and a dynamic speaker at TEDxPortland. She has an honorary doctorate degree from the University of Portland and by the way, she happens to have Down syndrome.

Local celebrity, Devon Adelman, will be sharing some of her experiences in the last year: speaking at the United Nations as part of the World Down Syndrome Day Conference, visiting the White House as part of Michelle Obama's Reach Higher Summit, participating in Special Olympics Unified Relay, and starting college.
